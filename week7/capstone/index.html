Who are we?

Hello and welcome! We are Colorful Heart VG! An orginization commited to serving the community in every way we can. Founded by our founder in the year 2020, we've help countless live through our efforts. We act as a hub for community outreach, individual assitance, and disaster relief efforts. Our goal is too give back to the lovely people of this community by bringing together those from all walks of life in hopes for a brighter tomorrow.
